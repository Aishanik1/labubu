About Labubu Shooter

Labubu Shooter is a hand-crafted HTML5 casual game created by a small indie team who simply love jumping, shooting, and pastel color palettes. Our goal is to deliver lightweight, instantly playable fun without installs, logins, or micro-transactions.

Our Mission

  • Provide 100 % free, family-friendly entertainment.
  • Keep the codebase open for educational use.
  • Respect user privacy — no personal data is collected.
